**Research Status:** 🟡 In Progress

**Last Updated:** 20/06/2026

**Primary Research Question:**

> How did Kubernetes' rapid growth create coordination costs within its open-source ecosystem?

# Research Objectives

This case study aims to investigate:

* How Kubernetes scaled.
* What coordination challenges emerged.
* How governance evolved.
* What technical and social bottlenecks appeared.
* Which mechanisms reduced friction.

---

# Initial Hypothesis

Hypothesis:

As Kubernetes grew, coordination costs increased faster than contributor capacity, requiring increasingly sophisticated governance and automation mechanisms.
